Once we have gathered all the necessary information, our team of skilled technicians will conduct a thorough inspection of your vehicle using state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment. At our auto repair shop, we understand that your time is valuable, which is why we are committed to providing efficient service without compromising on quality. Whether you're in need of routine maintenance or unexpected repairs, you can trust us to handle your vehicle with care and expertise every step of the way.

FAQS

How long will it take for my vehicle to be ready ?

Your vehicle will be ready in the next 2 days or as soon as the parts have arrived ( if needed ) , but there is no set time as there are many things to consider when determining how long it will take.

Do I have to call or email first before showing up to your location ?

Yes , please call and make an appointment as our schedule is quiet busy at times and we dont take 'surprise' customers without making an appointment first.

Do I provide the parts that I want replace when bringing in my vehicle ?

Yes but it is not really needed as we will provide them for you if you cannot. Keep in mind if we have to provide the parts and we are out of stock at the time it will take a longer period of time.

Will I recieve a cost estimate beforehand ?

Yes you will , we strive to be upfront and honest with our clients to give them the best deals we possibly can!

Do I pay after the job is done ?

You can pay before or after , but if the payment is not done after completion of the job your vehicle will be held until you decide to pay.

What vehicle models have to previously worked on ?
