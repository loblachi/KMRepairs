Auto Diagnostic & Repairs Services

Brakes

We are leaders in the field of automotive repair and vehicle servicing. We have qualified mechanics (technicians) who stand willing and ready to assist you with any brakes related issues.

Services

We can handle both minor and major services for all makes and models of cars, SUVs, and bakkies. So no matter what type of vehicle you drive, we have you covered. And by regularly servicing your car, you can not only keep it running smoothly but also extend its lifespan and avoid costly repairs down the road.

Clutches

One of the most troublesome parts of a car is the clutch, as the condition of the clutch depends on a lot of factors, including the way we drive.Making sure your clutch is in perfect condition is important because your safety depends on it. An improper working clutch could result in an accident in a worst-case scenario.

Starters

A faulty starter motor can cause safety hazards, such as stalling in traffic or sudden engine failure.A well-maintained starter motor is more likely to work efficiently, reducing the risk of problems like slow cranking or failed starts.

Suspension

A properly maintained suspension system significantly contributes to your safety on the road. It helps keep your vehicle stable and balanced, especially during sudden manoeuvres, turns, and emergency stops so get it checked out before its too late.

Cambelts

The cam or timing belt is the only means of keeping the different aspects of a running engine in the proper sequence.As with any component, cambelts are affected by age and use. Age causes rubber to perish and weaken, which we will be happy to replace.

Wheel Bearings

The wheel bearings are essential in the operation of your vehicle; they allow friction-reduced movement and rotation of the hub assembly, and thereby provides smooth tyre and wheel rotation. Think for a moment what would happen if you are driving along, with your loved ones, and a wheel bearing fails. When a wheel bearing goes bad, you are in for some trouble.

Alternator

Dim lights? Bad alternator sound? Battery not charging? Your alternator may be going bad!Come to KM Repairs for a checkup and get that replaced before it causes more problems.

Turbo

Think your turbo is going bad by your vehicles poor performance ? We got you , go on and give us a call and set an appointment and you're already on the right track on getting your car back to its full potential.

Injector

It is rare that the fuel injection system needs to be serviced. Today’s fuels keep injectors clean. If a problem does arise, we can diagnose the fuel injection system to find out what needs to be repaired. Injectors can fail due to leaking or an electrical issue.

Steering

You Feel A Vibration Or Shake When Driving? Dripping Fluid Under Your Car ? Your Steering Wheel Is Off-Center? Better get that fixed before an accident occurs.

Batteries

The last thing you need is to be stuck with a vehicle that will not start because of a dead battery. The electrical energy, stored in the battery, is what helps the vehicle to start and continue running.

Performance

Is your car getting slower by the day ? Bring it over to KM Repairs for a checkup or even get some upgrades for a better performance.

Fuel Consumption

Fuel price hikes are an integral part of your existence and your budget. Many people do their best to stretch their budget each month, and your vehicle just keeps taking more than it should ? Bring it over and we'll see whats the problem and make it a problem of the past!

Gearbox

Your vehicle might struggle to switch between gears, or in severe cases, it might not be able to switch at all.Your car also slips in and out of gear under load, like going up a hill. Does this sound like your vehicle? Call us ASAP

Warning Lights

Your car's proverbial brain will figure out an impending problem before you do and send you a warning more often than not. There are sensors and warning lights in new cars that alert you to tire pressure levels and the washer fluid level.Dont know how to make them go away? You know where to come.

Diagnostics

Modern day automobiles come standard with sophisticated on-board computers and immense electronics. This is where class-leading diagnostic tools are utilised to identify and accurately pinpoint the problem on your vehicle and the source of the issue.

Exhausts

Exhaust Leak? You see strange smoke colors coming from the exhaust ? You Have Rust On Or In The Exhaust System ? Our well trained and trusted technicians are waiting on standby!
